Timebox: How to use it to control your time

Learn how to use timeboxing to manage your productivity

Timeboxing consist of three section: dumping, prioritizing and scheduling

Brain Dump

  • Used to offload your brain to make it easier to focus on each of the tasks.
  • The context, big picres, ideas or little details that might be easy to forget but which might be useful for the task.
  • Leave everything on the paper in the evening before the new day or/and first thing in the morning

Top Priorities

  • There is only so much can be effectivly done in one day.
  • Go through Brain Dump section and extract the most important topic that is critical to deliver for that day to be counted as successful.
  • That is our first priority. It doesn't matter if it's big or small, is it related to work or personal matters, whats matters if it's critical to finish today

Scheduling

  • The only thing we left to do is to allocate time on tasks we want to finish today. To be crazy about it, don't fill the whole thing, it's only soo many hours in that section so you have option to adjust scheduling to comfortable to you time.
  • Don't forget the priorities, leave more time for the top priority as it is the critical things to do
  • Plan your breaks as well as family time and home chores Having all of these in your schedul will help focus on everything without thinking too much on time spend on it
  • The last but not least thing about scheduling, is that looking at priorities, it is important to remember that not each task should be finished today, you can spend 30 minutes on cleaning the house a day isntead of making it the whole day operation and as result it won't burden you as much as it could
